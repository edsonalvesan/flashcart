What is FlashCart?

FlashCart is a mobile friendly shopping cart template for your online store.

It can be customized for your business and is very easy to manage.

Made with ♥ for small businesses


♥ Customers' orders are communicated through WhatsApp.

♥ Inventory managed with Google Sheets.

♥ Optimized for mobile phone display.

♥ Can be setup within a day.

♥ The shop can run on your custom domain, (yourshopname).com or (yourshopname).in or whatever you like.
